About

Ship Cargo Analysis

Ship Cargo Analysis is a web application designed to manage and analyze shipments, routes, and related data. This platform helps businesses streamline their logistics and optimize shipping processes.

Key Features

  • Efficient shipment tracking and management
  • Route optimization for cost-effective shipping
  • Warehouse and supplier management

How It Works

Ship Cargo Analysis allows users to:

  1. Track shipments from departure to arrival
  2. Optimize routes based on distance and duration
  3. Manage warehouse inventory and suppliers
